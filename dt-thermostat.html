Design Technology Thermostat

duration:
Jan-Jun 2016

skills used:
Tinkercad, 3D Printing, Soldering

project introduction:

Electrical appliances are used all year round in schools, offices, and homes. As a result of our frequent usage of these appliances, infrastructures like the aformentioned consume large amounts of energy on a yearly basis. Through this project, I aim to solve this problem by designing a smart thermostat that can be used in a proposed classroom to minimize our energy consumption, thus helping to reduce expenses on energy bills.
