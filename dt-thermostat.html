Design Technology Thermostat Project

In my high school Design Technology class, I designed and made a 3D printed thermostat that shows a light indicating the temperature in the room.

To show the temperature in the room, I learned new techniques like soldering, wiring, and digital rendering. I ended up with a functional final product that showed either a blue or red light depending on the temperature!


Tools used: 3D Printer, TinkerCAD, soldering iron
