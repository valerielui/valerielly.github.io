Quarantine Survey Form

duration:
Apr 2020

skills used:
HTML/ CSS

introduction:

Over quarantine, I came up with the idea of learning to code and then coding my own portfolio website (this website!!!) out of frustration with existing website-building platforms. At the time, I had absolutely zero experience with HTML and CSS, but nevertheless, I was determined to make my website a reality.

That being said, I started learning HTML and CSS through a certification course from FreeCodeCamp and eventually grasped the basics of HTML and CSS! As I was finishing up the course, I had to work on a few projects that fulfilled user-profiles and this survey form was one of them. Being inspired by the context of the world during that time, I created this survey form asking questions about food cravings in hopes that I would be able to visit these restaurants soon enough.

Unfortunately, due to the state of the world, I have been unable to fulfill these cravings. However, I hope you enjoy interacting with it as I am incredibly proud of this form I created with my ultra-beginner coding skills at the time!
