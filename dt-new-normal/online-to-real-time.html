Online to Real Time

FALL 2021 | Photos by: Amanda Chou, Peter Gastis, Polina Past, Bonnie Tin Sum Lee, Simon Park & Jessica Wild

College sports are an integral part of most universities in the United States, and USC is no exception. Only available online through streaming services and cable TV the past two semesters, students have packed stands, shouted chants and cheered on their fellow classmates from the sidelines. From Galen Center, the Los Angeles Memorial Coliseum and Uytengsu Aquatics Center to away games at rival stadiums to celebrating Conquest! on campus, here are perspectives Daily Trojan photographers captured of the return of in-person sports.
