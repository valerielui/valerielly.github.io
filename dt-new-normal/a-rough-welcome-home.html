A Rough Welcome Home

9/01/2021

Since students returned to campus in August, they had to adapt to the new state of a recovering University. From weekly coronavirus tests to learning how to navigate the University Park Campus, students in this Daily Trojan photo essay have experienced the issues of USC’s new normal.

Photos by: Amanda Chou, Sarah Cortina, Andrew Kerner, Vincent Leo, Talha Rafique & Eric Yeich
